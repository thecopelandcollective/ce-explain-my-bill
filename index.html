Monthly Electric Use

This area of your bill shows you how much electricity your home used over the last 13 months. When the weather gets very hot or cold, it forces your cooling and heating systems to work harder and use more energy, even if you adjust your thermostat. Your energy use history can help you:

  • Identify high-use months and discover ways to reduce your energy use during these times
  • Forecast your energy use
  • Manage your energy use
  • Detect fluctuations
Monthly Energy Summary

Electric use is measured in kilowatt-hours (kWh). Your bill is your actual use multiplied by your rate.

For more information on your specific rate, see our Energy Charges Explained page.

Payment Coupon

Want the fastest and simplest way to pay your bill? Skip the hassle and pay online. Or get your bill by email simply by switching preferences in your online profile. If you are paying by mail, simply detach this payment coupon and send it with your payment.

Account Information

Here you'll find the bill month and service dates covered in your bill. It's important to understand that the number of days in the billing period can impact your bill. Fewer days can reduce your energy use and lower your bill, while more days can have the opposite effect.

Our Energy Dashboard can help you better understand your energy use patterns over the past 13 months.

Rate Information

Meter Information

In this section, you'll find specific information about your electric meter, including when it was read and how much energy you used. If you want to learn more about your energy use, our dashboard can give you more details.
