Explore your bill

Select the bill type you receive from the choices below, and we’ll show you where to locate key details on your bill.

Total Electric Use

This area of your bill shows you how much electricity your home used over the last 13 months. You can use your energy use history to:

  • Detect fluctuations
  • Identify high-use months and determine future energy saving plans
  • Forecast your energy use
  • Manage your energy use
Monthly Energy Summary

Electric use is measured in kilowatt-hours and then billed according to your actual use. For more information on your specific rate, see our Energy Charges Explained page.

Payment Coupon

Detach your payment coupon from the bottom of the bill so you can include it with your payment. Or pay now online. Try the convenience of receiving your bill via email. It’s easy to make the switch in your online profile.

Account Information

Use this section to see how many days are in the billing period and your specific rate. More days in the billing period could result in more energy used and a higher bill, while fewer days can cut your monthly energy use and bill. Log in now to check your energy use online.

Monthly Charges Details

This is where we keep you informed about everything included in your rate. If you have questions, call us at 1-800-477-5050 or monitor your account online. Log in to get started.

Rate Information

The amount of energy you use and when you use it determines your rate.
